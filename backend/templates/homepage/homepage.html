MindShift its a toolkit that help you to get more control about your thoughts and actions.

Features

Track Thoughts

Allows you to track your thoughts over the time.

Thoughts Charting

Observe the relationships and characteristics of your thoughts graphically.

Search And Filter Thoughts

Allows you to smart search and filter your thoughts easily, intuitively and accurately.

Dangerous Thought Alerts

You will be notified when a thought related to the emotions of sadness or anger is becoming too repetitive or constant in your daily life.
